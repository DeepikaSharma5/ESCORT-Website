University students' guidance system

An application to help university students to solve their day today university issues

Get Started

Research Scope

Literature Survey

A chat-bot application to contact university administration about issues/ doubts or clarifications - Minimize the interaction between university administration and students, which normally costs a lot of time. Providing a chat bot where students can find a solution via a chat-bot by asking questions in their preferred language.

A solution recommendation system for psychological issues to university students - provide a better solution for the issues in psychology, to the university students in preferred language.

Career Guidance and IT Modules related Question Answering System for IT students - Building an IT career guidance Question Answering system along with the QA system on IT modules

A system to predict and analyze students' performance and recommend suitable Learning resources to the students - Improving students' performance by recommending suitable learning materials and helping them to find relevant jobs.

Research Problem

  • University students and administration facing issues while communicating because, lack of time and language difference.
  • University students are unable to receive easy solution for psychological issues because of unawareness and communication issues
  • Students want to clarify answers regarding career-guidance and modules related questions
  • University students are required to analyze their performance to determine their weaknesses. 

Research Gap

  • Problem with existing technology.
  • No system available to give administrative related solutions for university students.
  • No system available to give psychological related solution for university students.
  • Career-specific and IT-specific systems are not exist.
  • Recommending learning resources by conducting online quizzes.

Research Objectives

  • Give solutions for the doubts/ issues or clarifications related to university administration.
  • Recommend exercises and activities in preferred language for psychological issues.
  • Recommends mentors, courses & certifications for students based on their preference.
  • Predicts student performance and recommends suitable learning materials.

Methodology

The Proposed system will be carried out under 4 components

  • A chat-bot application to contact university administration about issues/ doubts or clarifications - Data Collection with google form, Implement model using NLP, Keras library to build NLP model, dataset with more than 2800 questions and answers in English, Tamil, and Thanglish, Text-preprocessing
  • Recommending Solutions for Psychological Issues to University Students - Dataset collection using a google form, tokenization, keyword extraction, stemming and similarity calculation using NLTK, Sklearn, and python libraries. Topic modelling with English, Tamil, Sinhala and Thanglish using Gensim.
  • IT Career Guidance and modules related Question Answering System - Data collection from blogs, articles and field experts. Python, FAISS as document store (Vector database) to store data, NLP methodologies such as normalization, text-processing are processed.
  • A system to predict and analyze students' performance and recommend suitable Learning - Data collection, support vector machines algorithm, decision tree classifier algorithm, random forest classifier algorithm, gaussian naive bayes algorithm
